About Me

Compassionate Care, Expert Medicine

I am a board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating cardiovascular conditions. My approach combines cutting-edge medical technology with personalized, patient-centered care.

I believe in preventive medicine and work closely with patients to develop comprehensive treatment plans that address both immediate concerns and long-term heart health.

My Specialties

Areas of Expertise

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive procedures including angioplasty, stenting, and cardiac catheterization.

  • Coronary Angioplasty
  • Stent Placement
  • Cardiac Catheterization

Preventive Cardiology

Risk assessment and prevention strategies for heart disease before symptoms appear.

  • Risk Assessment
  • Lifestyle Modification
  • Cholesterol Management

Electrophysiology

Diagnosis and treatment of heart rhythm disorders and arrhythmias.

  • Arrhythmia Treatment
  • Pacemaker Implantation
  • Ablation Procedures

Echocardiography

Advanced cardiac imaging for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • Stress Echo
  • 3D Echocardiography
  • Doppler Studies
